About SunBot Robotics

Empowering the Next Generation of Innovators

Our Mission

At SunBot Robotics, we are committed to inspiring young minds through hands-on STEM education. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to explore, create, and innovate in a supportive and engaging environment.

Our mission is to:

  • Foster creativity and critical thinking skills
  • Prepare students for future careers in STEM fields
  • Build confidence through hands-on projects
  • Create a community of young innovators

Our Vision

We envision a future where every child has access to quality STEM education and the tools they need to succeed in an increasingly technology-driven world.

SunBot Robotics aims to be a leading STEM education provider in Edmonton and beyond, recognized for our innovative teaching methods, dedicated instructors, and successful student outcomes.

Why Choose SunBot Robotics?

Expert Instructors

Our team consists of experienced educators and engineers passionate about STEM education.

Hands-On Learning

Students learn by doing, building, and programming real robots and projects.

Small Class Sizes

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student.

Competition Ready

Our programs prepare students for FLL and other robotics competitions.
