Comprehensive STEM Programs for Ages 6-15

Robotics Coding Class

Age: 6-9 years Duration: 10 weeks Starting at $299

Course Overview

Our Robotics Coding Class introduces young learners to the exciting world of robotics and programming. Students will learn the fundamentals of block-based coding while building and programming their own robots.

What Students Will Learn

  • Basic programming concepts using visual block coding
  • Robot assembly and construction techniques
  • Problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Teamwork and collaboration skills
  • Introduction to sensors and motors

Course Structure

Weekly Schedule: 1.5 hours per session, once per week

Class Size: Maximum 8 students per class

Materials: All robotics kits and materials provided

Competition Challenge Class

Age: 8-12 years Duration: 12 weeks Starting at $349

Course Overview

Designed for students who are ready to take on competitive robotics challenges, this class prepares participants for FIRST® LEGO® League (FLL) competitions. Students will work in teams to solve real-world problems through robotics.

What Students Will Learn

  • Advanced robotics programming and strategy
  • FLL competition rules and mission planning
  • Engineering design process
  • Project research and presentation skills
  • Team dynamics and leadership
  • Time management and competition preparation

Course Structure

Weekly Schedule: 2 hours per session, twice per week

Class Size: Maximum 6 students per class (organized into teams)

Materials: Competition-grade robotics kits included

Competition: Includes registration for local FLL events
