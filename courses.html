Our Courses

FIRST® LEGO® League Programs for Ages 6-14

FLL Explore

Age: 6-10 years CAD $40/hr

Promotions: Please contact us for special offers and discounts

Course Overview

FIRST® LEGO® League Explore (formerly FLL Jr.) introduces young students to the exciting world of STEM through hands-on exploration. Teams work together to build and program a LEGO model that addresses a real-world challenge theme.

Real Classroom Experience

What Students Will Learn

  • Basic robotics and engineering concepts
  • Simple programming using LEGO WeDo or SPIKE Essential
  • Teamwork and collaboration skills
  • Problem-solving through hands-on building
  • Presentation and communication skills
  • Exploration of real-world themes and challenges

Course Structure

Program Format: Team-based learning experience

Class Size: Maximum 8 students per class (organized into teams of 2-4)

Materials: LEGO Education kits and materials provided

Showcase: Teams present their projects at a local showcase event

FLL Challenge

Age: 9-14 years CAD $40/hr

Promotions: Please contact us for special offers and discounts

Course Overview

FIRST® LEGO® League Challenge is the most advanced level of FLL programs. Students design, build, and program autonomous robots to complete missions on a themed playing field. Teams also research and develop innovative solutions to real-world problems, then present their findings to judges.

Real Classroom Experience

What Students Will Learn

  • Advanced robotics programming and strategy
  • FLL Challenge competition rules and mission planning
  • Engineering design process and robot optimization
  • Research methodology and innovation project development
  • Team dynamics, leadership, and collaboration
  • Presentation skills and technical communication
  • Time management and competition preparation

Course Structure

Program Format: Competition-focused team training

Class Size: Maximum 6 students per class (organized into teams of 4-6)

Materials: Competition-grade LEGO SPIKE Prime or EV3 kits included

Competition: Includes registration for local and regional FLL Challenge events

Components: Robot Game (robotics), Innovation Project (research), and Core Values (teamwork)
