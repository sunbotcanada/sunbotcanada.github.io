About SunBot Robotics

SunBot Robotics is dedicated to providing high-quality STEM education for children aged 6-14. Our mission is to inspire creativity, foster problem-solving skills, and prepare the next generation of innovators through FIRST® LEGO® League programs.

Located in Edmonton, Canada, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that combines robotics, coding, and engineering principles to create engaging learning experiences for young students.
